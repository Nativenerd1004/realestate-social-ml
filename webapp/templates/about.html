Pipeline Summary

  • 5 real Kaggle datasets — 1.9M+ total training records
  • Datasets loaded into PostgreSQL 18 via SQLAlchemy for processing
  • 8 classifiers trained per tab with 80/20 stratified split
  • SMOTE applied to Tab 1 (2.85% minority class → balanced)
  • Best model per tab selected by F1 score — XGBoost wins 3/4
  • Social Media tab: F1=0.8762, ROC-AUC=0.9465 (best in suite)
  • Market Position covers 54 US states + 9 Canadian provinces
  • Deployed to Cloudflare Pages (pipeline) + Render.com (web app)
